MeSomb WooCommerce is an easy to integrate mobile payment (Mobile Money or Orange Money) on Woo Commerce.

== Description ==

MeSomb WooCommerce is a fast and easy way to integrate mobile payment (Mobile Money or Orange Money) in your Woo Commerce shop.
This plugin relies on the Hachther MeSomb payment system, and for the moment is only available in Cameroon for the moment.


== Installation ==

- Register on Hachther MeSomb [Link](https://mesomb.hachther.com/signup)
- Once registered, create an application for your product [Link](https://mesomb.hachther.com/en/applications/add/)
- Upload the MeSomb WooCommerce plugin to your blog, activate the MeSomb payment in WooCommerce payment settings, and set up your application key.

1, 2, 3: You're done!
